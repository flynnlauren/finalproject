  • Rachel Green ran off the day of her marriage and moved to New York City where she meets up with her old high school best friend Monica Geller and her dorky older brother Ross Geller and the rest of their friends. She moves in with Monica and becomes a member of the rest of the gang. Eventually Ranchel and Ross have a on again off again relationship with Ross and a daughter together Emma.
  • Monica Geller who is Rachel Green's high school best friend lives in her grandmother's apartment in New York city along with her older brother Ross and three friends. Monica is known for her over compulsive disorder of cleaning, her competitiveness, and stunning charm of always being right. Monica eventually marries Chandler Bing where the viewers get to see their loving and humorous relationship grow throughout the show.
  • Chandler Bing is your average funny, sacracstic friend who will always be there if you need to hear a horrible joke or just a hug to help get you through your day. Chandler is Ross's college best friend and later becomes Monica's husband. Chandler's roommate is Joey which they both live right across the hallway from Monica and Rachel.
  • Ross Geller, the only person you will ever meet to have his PHD and gets to play with dinosaurs. Ross is a devoted paleontologist and Monica's older nerdy broter. Ross is constantly in an on again off again relationship with Rachel, and he is the man known for having the most marriages. Ross just has bad luck with the ladies but is an all around sweet heart.
  • Phoebe Buffay is not your average ordinary friend. She can sometimes see into the future, or know that her mom's spirit is trapped inside a cat's body. Phoebe finds her long lost brother and eventually has his babies. It sounds weirder than you think, but she became a surrogate mother for her brother and his wife. Phoebe is well known for her use of words especially when it comes to the "phalanges".
